Who am I?

I am certainly not someone who believes in using overused adjectives, and yet I was never any good with long, confusing sentences. So I shall describe myself in crisp phrases. I'm very good at the things that I do. I'm even better at coming up with creative excuses about why I'm bad at the things I avoid. However, I dislike avoiding things, so I usually get better at them rather quickly. I like to help people, and love it when people help me. I maintain that I'm funny, yet some of my closest friends have suggested that I'm actually a joke myself. Thankfully, I don't let their criticism daunt me. I pay attention to details, just not the discouraging ones.

What do I do ?

I'm rather lazy. But then, all the best people always are. I try to find things I'm tired of doing myself, and attempt to teach computers to do them for me. I honestly believe that the smarter they get, the lazier we may allow ourselves to successfully become. But as any self-respecting adult would, I also curate a vast collection of impressive words to surprise unsuspecting victims with. Here are some of my favourites:
